# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Getting Started

### Q: How do I start?
A: Use `/profile` to create your account and Tea Card, then submit work for tasks using `/sendwork`.

### Q: How do I earn XP?
A: Submit work for admin-defined tasks. When your work is approved, you receive the task's base XP plus any optional bonus XP from the admin.

## Roles

### Q: What are roles?
A: Roles reflect your progress and determine your reward multiplier:
- **Sprout Leaf** 🍃 — 200 XP, 0.75x multiplier
- **Green Leaf** 🌿 — 650 XP, 1.3x multiplier
- **Golden Leaf** 🍂 — manual promotion, 2.5x multiplier

### Q: Can I lose my role?
A: No, roles never auto-demote. Only an admin can manually change your role.

## Rewards

### Q: How does the reward pool work?
A: A monthly pool of $2500 (default) is distributed among eligible users. Your share is calculated as: monthly XP x role multiplier, divided by the total weighted XP of all eligible users.

### Q: Am I eligible for rewards?
A: Only if you have a role (200+ total XP). Users without a role are excluded from the monthly reward pool.

## Work Submission

### Q: How do I submit work?
A: Use `/sendwork`, select a task from the dropdown, then fill in the URL and optional description.

### Q: What is Bonus XP?
A: Extra XP that an admin can optionally award during work approval, on top of the task's base XP.

## Special Roles

### Q: What is a Local Lead?
A: Local Leaders are community managers with a special Discord role. They can submit monthly reports via `/localleadreport`.

## Technical

### Q: The bot isn't responding.
A: Check if you're in the correct channel and try again in a few minutes. Contact an admin if issues persist.

### Q: I didn't receive my DM notification.
A: Enable DMs from server members in your Discord privacy settings.

### Q: Can I be banned?
A: Yes, for serious or repeated rule violations. Bans are issued by admins.
