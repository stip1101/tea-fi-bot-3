# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Getting Started

### Q: How do I start?
A: Go to <#1397567024837824573> and use `/profile` to create your account and Tea Card, then submit work for tasks using `/sendwork`.

### Q: How do I earn XP?
A: Submit work for admin-defined tasks. When your work is approved, you receive the task's base XP plus any optional bonus XP from the reviewer.

## Program Structure

### Q: Is Phase 3 a continuation of Phase 2?
A: No. Phase 3 introduces a redesigned, performance-based structure with fixed reward pools and transparent XP progression. Previous roles do not automatically transfer. All contributors progress under the new system.

### Q: Will my previous contribution be ignored?
A: No. Phase 2 participation remains recognized at a strategic level. However, monthly rewards and level progression in Phase 3 are determined solely by verified performance within the new framework.

### Q: How long is a performance cycle?
A: Each cycle runs for one month. At the end of the cycle, XP is calculated and rewards are distributed.

## Roles

### Q: What are roles?
A: Roles reflect your progress and determine your reward multiplier:
- **Seed** — Starting level, no multiplier, excluded from rewards
- **Sprout Leaf** 🍃 — 200 XP, 0.75x multiplier
- **Green Leaf** 🌿 — 650 XP, 1.3x multiplier
- **Golden Leaf** 🍂 — 1,500 XP + team approval, 2.5x multiplier

### Q: Can I lose my role?
A: No, roles never auto-demote. Only an admin can manually change your role.

### Q: How do I enter the Golden Leaf tier?
A: Selection is based on consistency, ecosystem alignment, quality of output, and measurable impact. You need at least 1,500 XP and then the team personally selects the best contributors.

## Rewards

### Q: How does the reward pool work?
A: The Leaf Way pool is $2,500/month in $TEA. Your share is calculated as: monthly XP × role multiplier, divided by the total weighted XP of all eligible users.

### Q: Am I eligible for rewards?
A: Only if you have a role (200+ total XP). Seed users are excluded from the monthly reward pool.

### Q: Can the reward pools change?
A: Reward pools are fixed and announced before each cycle. Adjustments, if any, are communicated transparently prior to cycle start.

### Q: How are rewards calculated?
A: Rewards are distributed from fixed monthly pools. Allocation is based on XP with role multipliers applied.

## Work Submission

### Q: How do I submit work?
A: Use `/sendwork`, select a task from the dropdown, then fill in the URL and optional description. You can also click "Submit Work" on your `/profile`.

### Q: What is Bonus XP?
A: Extra XP that a reviewer can optionally award during work approval, on top of the task's base XP. Bonus XP is displayed separately in your profile.

### Q: What qualifies as "ecosystem-aligned activity"?
A: Ecosystem alignment may include product usage, governance participation, on-chain interaction, and campaign involvement.

## Local Leaders

### Q: What is a Local Lead?
A: Local Leaders are community managers who build Tea-Fi community in their region. They have a separate reward pool of $1,000/month.

### Q: How do I become a Local Lead?
A: Apply here: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe9kFfvRomA5ynJznvJWxEoOPMLjwNww1T5qdkl6YM49VNDWw/viewform?usp=header

### Q: Can I participate in both Creator and Local Leader roles?
A: Yes, if performance standards are maintained. However, evaluation and reward logic for each role are structured independently.

## Technical

### Q: The bot isn't responding.
A: Check if you're in the correct channel and try again in a few minutes. Contact an admin if issues persist.

### Q: I didn't receive my DM notification.
A: Enable DMs from server members in your Discord privacy settings.

### Q: Can I be banned?
A: Yes, for serious or repeated rule violations. Bans are issued by admins.
