# Local Leaders

## What are Local Leaders?

Local Leaders expand Tea-Fi regionally and build strong community hubs. They have a separate track with their own reward pool.

## Reward Pool

- **Monthly pool:** 250,000 $TEA
- Distributed manually at the end of each month based on report review

## How to Become a Local Leader

Apply here: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe9kFfvRomA5ynJznvJWxEoOPMLjwNww1T5qdkl6YM49VNDWw/viewform?usp=header

You need the Local Lead Discord role to submit reports.

## Contribution Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Onboarding & Guidance | Local user onboarding, education, first-touch support |
| Community Operations | Moderation, coordination, maintaining local channels |
| Local Initiatives | AMAs, Spaces, meetups, regional activations |

## Monthly Reports

Local Leaders submit monthly reports via the `/localleadreport` command.

You don't need to submit your work regularly for these tasks. Towards the end of the month, submit a report covering your monthly contributions.

### Report Contents
- **Report document link** — link to your monthly report (Google Doc or similar, must be accessible to administrators)
- **Comment** (optional) — additional notes or context

### Limits
- One report per user per month
- Requires the Local Lead Discord role

## How to Submit

1. Type `/localleadreport` in any channel
2. Provide your report document link
3. Optionally add a comment
4. Submit

After reviewing your report, you will receive a notification about the result.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I become a Local Lead?**
A: Fill out the application form: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe9kFfvRomA5ynJznvJWxEoOPMLjwNww1T5qdkl6YM49VNDWw/viewform?usp=header

**Q: Can I submit more than one report per month?**
A: No, the limit is one report per user per month.

**Q: Can I participate in both Creator and Local Leader roles?**
A: Yes, if performance standards are maintained. However, evaluation and reward logic for each role are structured independently.

**Q: What should the report include?**
A: Prepare a document covering your community management activities for the month (onboarding, moderation, events, etc.) and share the link.
