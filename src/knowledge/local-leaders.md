# Local Leaders

## What are Local Leaders?

Local Leaders are community managers with a special Discord role. They serve as regional or community leads within the TeaFi Program.

## Monthly Reports

Local Leaders can submit monthly reports via the `/localleadreport` command.

### Report Contents
- **Google Doc link** — link to the monthly report document
- **Comment** (optional) — additional notes or context

### Limits
- One report per user per month
- Requires the LOCAL_LEAD_ROLE_ID Discord role

## How to Use

1. Type `/localleadreport` in the designated channel
2. Provide your Google Doc link with the monthly report
3. Optionally add a comment
4. Submit

## FAQ

**Q: How do I become a Local Lead?**
A: Local Lead is a special role assigned by admins. Contact the admin team if you're interested.

**Q: Can I submit more than one report per month?**
A: No, the limit is one report per user per month.

**Q: What should the report include?**
A: Prepare a Google Doc covering your community management activities for the month and share the link.
