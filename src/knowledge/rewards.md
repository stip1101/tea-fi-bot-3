# Reward System

## Monthly Reward Pools

The Tea-Fi Program has two separate monthly reward pools:

### Leaf Way Pool
- **Amount:** 600,000 $TEA per month
- **For:** Content creators (Sprout Leaf, Green Leaf, Golden Leaf)
- **Based on:** XP earned that month × role multiplier

### Local Leaders Pool
- **Amount:** 250,000 $TEA per month
- **For:** Local Leaders
- **Based on:** Monthly report review, distributed manually

## Leaf Way Eligibility

Only users with a role participate in the Leaf Way reward pool:
- **Seed** (< 200 XP) — excluded from rewards
- **Sprout Leaf** (200+ XP) — eligible with 0.75x multiplier
- **Green Leaf** (650+ XP) — eligible with 1.3x multiplier
- **Golden Leaf** (1,500+ XP + approval) — eligible with 2.5x multiplier

## Role Multipliers

| Role | Multiplier |
|------|-----------|
| Sprout Leaf 🍃 | 0.75x |
| Green Leaf 🌿 | 1.3x |
| Golden Leaf 🍂 | 2.5x |

## Calculation Formula

1. **Weighted XP** = monthly XP × role multiplier
2. **Point price** = Monthly Pool / Total Weighted XP (sum of all eligible users)
3. **Your reward** = your Weighted XP × point price

### Example

Pool: 600,000 $TEA. Three eligible users this month:
- User A: 100 monthly XP, Green Leaf (1.3x) → 130 weighted XP
- User B: 80 monthly XP, Sprout Leaf (0.75x) → 60 weighted XP
- User C: 50 monthly XP, Golden Leaf (2.5x) → 125 weighted XP

Total weighted XP: 315. Point price: 600,000 / 315 = 1,904.76 $TEA

- User A reward: 130 × 1,904.76 = 247,619 $TEA
- User B reward: 60 × 1,904.76 = 114,286 $TEA
- User C reward: 125 × 1,904.76 = 238,095 $TEA

## Monthly XP

Only XP earned in the current month counts for reward distribution. Sources:
- Base XP from approved work tasks
- Bonus XP awarded by reviewers during approval

## Checking Your Estimate

Use `/rewards` to view your current monthly reward estimate.

## FAQ

**Q: When are rewards distributed?**
A: At the end of each monthly performance cycle.

**Q: Do I need a role to get rewards?**
A: Yes. Seed users (< 200 XP) are excluded from the Leaf Way pool.

**Q: Does my role multiplier change my XP?**
A: No, the multiplier only affects reward calculation. Your actual XP stays the same.

**Q: Can the reward pools change?**
A: Reward pools are fixed and announced before each cycle. Adjustments, if any, are communicated transparently prior to cycle start.
