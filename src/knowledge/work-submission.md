# Work Submission Guide

## How to Submit Work

### Method 1: /sendwork Command
Type `/sendwork` in the designated channel.

### Method 2: Profile Button
1. Use `/profile` to open your Tea Card
2. Click the **Submit Work** button

## Submission Process

### Step 1: Select a Task
Choose a task from the dropdown menu. Tasks are defined by admins and each has a fixed XP reward.

### Step 2: Fill the Modal
You'll see a form with two fields:

1. **URL** (required) — Link to your work (Twitter post, content, etc.)
2. **Description** (optional) — Brief description of what you did

## Review Process

After submission:
1. Your work appears in the review channel
2. Twitter links get automatic metrics tracking
3. AI analysis is performed on the submission
4. Admin reviews your work

## Results

**If Approved:**
- Base XP from the task is automatically awarded
- Admin may add optional bonus XP
- Notification sent to task logs and your DMs

**If Rejected:**
- No XP awarded
- Reason provided by admin
- Notification sent to task logs and your DMs

## Limits

- **Pending limit:** Maximum 3 pending works at a time
- **Submit cooldown:** 5 minutes between submissions

## FAQ

**Q: How long until my work is reviewed?**
A: Depends on admin availability. Check your pending works with `/profile`.

**Q: How many works can I have pending?**
A: Maximum 3 pending works at a time.

**Q: Can I submit the same work twice?**
A: No, duplicate submissions will be rejected.

**Q: How do I pick a task?**
A: Tasks are created by admins. Select from the available tasks in the dropdown when submitting work.
