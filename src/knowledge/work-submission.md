# Work Submission Guide

## How to Submit Work

### Method 1: /sendwork Command
Type `/sendwork` in any authorized channel.

### Method 2: Profile Button
1. Use `/profile` to open your Tea Card
2. Click the **Submit Work** button at the bottom

## Task Types

| Type | Description | XP |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Creative Content | Short-form creative pieces highlighting the product or ecosystem | up to 15 XP |
| UGC | User-generated content reflecting authentic interaction with Tea-Fi | up to 15 XP |
| Narrative Support | Content reinforcing key narratives and supporting campaigns | up to 25 XP |
| Article | In-depth written content with structured analysis or education | up to 30 XP |

## Submission Process

### Step 1: Select a Task
Choose a task from the dropdown menu. Each task has a defined XP reward.

### Step 2: Fill the Modal
You'll see a form with two fields:

1. **URL** (required) — Link to your work (Twitter post, article, content, etc.)
2. **Description** (optional) — Brief description of what you did

## Review Process

After submission:
1. Your work appears in the review channel
2. Twitter links get automatic metrics tracking
3. AI analysis is performed on the submission
4. A reviewer checks your work

## Results

**If Approved:**
- Base XP from the task is automatically awarded
- Reviewer may add optional Bonus XP for exceptional work
- You receive a DM notification with details about XP earned and quality score

**If Rejected:**
- No XP awarded
- Reason provided by the reviewer
- You receive a DM notification with feedback

## Limits

- **Pending limit:** Maximum 3 pending works at a time
- **Submit cooldown:** 5 minutes between submissions

## FAQ

**Q: How long until my work is reviewed?**
A: Depends on reviewer availability. Check your pending works with `/profile`.

**Q: How many works can I have pending?**
A: Maximum 3 pending works at a time.

**Q: Can I submit the same work twice?**
A: No, duplicate submissions will be rejected.

**Q: How do I pick a task?**
A: Tasks are created by admins. Select from the available tasks in the dropdown when submitting work.
